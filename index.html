Rajputi jewellery

The Rajput dynasties ruled between the 7th and 19th centuries and brought with them skilled craftsmen who introduced some exceptional gold jewellery designs. Originating from Rajasthan, Rajputana gold jewellery is one of the most exquisite styles of jewellery in India. The Rajputana style of jewellery is a reflection of its royal heritage and rich culture and remains an integral part of Rajasthani culture even today.
